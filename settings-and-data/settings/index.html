Settings

Customize Brainy to match your preferences and workflow:

General Settings

Database Location

  • Specifies where your database file (brainy.db) is stored
  • Contains all your files, cells, and study data
  • Useful for backup or moving your data to a different location
  • Changes take effect after restarting the app

Theme

  • Light — Bright interface for daytime use
  • Dark — Dark interface for comfortable viewing in low light
  • System — Automatically follows your operating system's theme preference

Zoom Level

  • Adjust the size of text and UI elements
  • Useful if you prefer larger or more compact text
  • Changes apply immediately

Sync & Data

Auto-Sync on Startup & Close

  • Enabled — Your data automatically syncs when you open and close the app
  • Disabled — Manual sync only (use Ctrl + Shift + Y to sync manually)
  • Helpful for keeping your data current across devices
  • See Cloud Sync for more information

Account Management

Sign Out

Log out of your current account while keeping local data intact.

Update Account Information

Change your email address or password associated with your Brainy account.

Delete Account

⚠️ Permanent action — Removes your account and all synced data from the server. Local data remains but sync functionality will be unavailable.
